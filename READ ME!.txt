---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
			               Controls!
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Up: W
Down: S
Left: A
Right: D
Shoot: Spacebar/Left Click
Interact: Left Click

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
			              How To Play!
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In Galactic Onslaught your objective is to kill the enemies, collect score & progress
through the levels to ultimately fight the boss at the end. Take flight and use the
controls above to manipulate the player and fight the enemies to powerup and defeat the
waves of horde that appear before you!
